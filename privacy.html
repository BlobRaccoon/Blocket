Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

When you use Blocket, we collect minimal data required to run the game:

  • Account Data: Username and hashed password for your login.
  • Game Progress: Data related to your items, stats, and achievements.
  • Log Data: IP addresses and browser info for security and to prevent hacking.

2. How We Use Data

Your data is only used to maintain your account, save your game progress, and protect the community from rule-breakers.

3. Third Parties

We do not sell your data to third parties. We only share information if required by law or to protect the safety of our users.
